Methodology

How it works

Three steps from raw data to clear outcome predictions.

01
We collect booth-level data
Official ECI voter rolls, 2001–2021 results by constituency, and real-time alliance signals — all from primary sources.
02
We simulate elections thousands of times
Each run models vote share by constituency, factoring in regional swings, alliance transfers, and incumbency effects.
03
We estimate likely outcomes
Seat projections with confidence intervals — not a single prediction, but a range that reflects genuine uncertainty.

About VakkuCheck

Election intelligence
built for everyone.

VakkuCheck is a civic intelligence platform built by NilamLabs LLC. We believe election data should be transparent, accessible, and useful — not locked behind jargon or paywalls.

🏺
The Kudavolai Inspiration

Named after the Kudavolai system — one of the world's earliest secret ballots, used in Tamil Nadu around 920 CE under the Chola dynasty at Uttiramerur. Ballot leaves (olai) were placed in a pot (kuda). Democracy has deep roots here.

🔬
Our Methodology

Bayesian vote-share model + Monte Carlo simulation (10,000 runs per update). We ingest official ECI electoral rolls, 2001–2021 result data, alliance signals, and regional swing patterns across 6 Tamil Nadu zones.

📊
What We Cover

Tamil Nadu 2026 Assembly Election (234 seats, April 23) and Puducherry 2026 (30 seats, April 9, counting May 4). Booth-level voter data, alliance tracking, AI signal agent, constituency drill-down.

🏢
NilamLabs LLC

An Earth/mineral-inspired data company. "Nilam" (நிலம்) means land or soil in Tamil — grounded, rooted, real. We build civic tools that connect people to data that matters.
